THE GRAND SOLON



Located squarely in the heart of Green Bay's arts district, your stay at The LUX allows you convenient access to our world-reknown shopping locales as well as our thriving theatre community. The cosmopolitan atmosphere doesnt end there. The LUX is very well known for our phonomenal day-spa; Our very own Dr. Susana Carney is in constant demand for her exceptional facial massage skills. Celebrities like Meryl Streep and Rihanna are known to fly into Green Bay quietly for routine visits with our world-class estheticians and massage therapists. Our Grand Salon is also a popular wedding location, and the LUX offers in-house catering for all formal events. Chef Paolo DeMarus, the head chef, oversees all meals ranging from breakfast to to wedding catering. Amenities offered to to guests at The Lux Hotel include:

  • In-Suite Hot tub
  • Room-Service
  • Complimentary Wifi
  • Indoor & Outdoor Pool access
  • Complimentary access to Gym & Sauna facilities
